About Dataset
This Eye Disease Dataset or Ocular Disease Dataset is a curated and preprocessed collection of fundus images designed for the classification of Six common ocular diseases and Normal fundus. The dataset addresses limitations such as class imbalance, low generalization, and overfitting that were encountered when models were trained on a single-source dataset.
To enhance diversity and robustness, this dataset merges images from five publicly available and widely used ophthalmic datasets:

ODIR-2019 (Ocular Disease Intelligent Recognition)
Mendeley Eye Disease Dataset
Kaggle Eye Diseases Classification Dataset
ARMD Curated Dataset 2023
Hypertension & Hypertensive Retinopathy Dataset
Each disease class was carefully sourced to ensure high-quality, class-specific representation:
Cataract: from Kaggle Eye Diseases Classification
AMD (Age-related Macular Degeneration): from ARMD Curated Dataset
Hypertensive Retinopathy: from ODIR-2019 and Hypertension dataset
Glaucoma, Myopia, Diabetic Retinopathy: from Mendeley
Normal: from ODIR-2019
